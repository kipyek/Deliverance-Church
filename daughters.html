Daughters of Faith

Introduction

Deliverance church international Kaplong daughters of faith is a warm fellowship comprising mothers who are members of the church. They usually meet on Tuesdays at church for their weekly fellowships. They also have a monthly DOF subregional rally which is conducted once in a month. This has brought in mothers from other denominations with the zeal to Christ more.

Vision

Empower women to transform the world through the love of Christ Jesus into abundance.

Mission

To promote spiritual, economic and social activities that respond to the needs of families, communities and the world through biblical principles.

Core Functions

  1. Mission- to reach out to the unsaved, needy and destitute and the neglected and a rejected with the Gospel and love of Christ.
  2. Capacity Building to empower Women to exploit their full God-given potential preaching Christ, caring for their families and communities.
  3. Poverty alleviation by implementing into grated projects.
  4. Promote collaboration and partnership.

Core Values

How to be a member of DOF

  1. Belong to a fellowship (DOF) in your local church.
  2. Register with a chairlady of your local church.
  3. Forward your registration to the National Office.

Why should you be a member

To be part of a network that empowers women to transform their lives into an abundant living through the love of Jesus Christ.
