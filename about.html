History

Deliverance church international Kaplong was started in1990 with four adults as pioneer members under the leadership of Bishop Dr. Philip Ngetich who was then serving as the lay pastor to the church. He was also teaching in a nearby primary school as an employee of Teachers Service Commission. He had a compelling call to serve God and he therefore led the establishment of the church. At inception, members conducted Sunday services at Chepkosiom primary school. In 1997, the church leadership decided to buy a half of an acre piece of land at Kaplong which is the current location of the church. The church ground was not immediately used for worship due to limited resources to build the church. At the time, economic situation of the country was unstable and most people lived in shortage. It was the time you could find the government providing relief food to almost every part of the country during dry spell. With such a situation, it was not possible to make significant progress even in church.

The church pastor and other leaders, despite such shortcomings, held their heads high and continued serving God and hoping that things would change and the house of God would one day grow to unimaginable proportions. That dream would start happening in 2002 when the church shifted to the church plot though unplanned. It was not planned because members woke up one day and they were told that the room they were using was no longer available and were required of them to look for another place. They indeed had to shift to an open church plot where they had to use a makeshift tent. Unknown to many members, that shift became the turning point for the church. The first foundation stone was laid around the same time leading to the construction of one of the best sanctuaries in the region. That was followed with the construction of the Sunday school sanctuary, extension of the church plot, fencing, construction of kitchen and acquisition of kitchen ware and equipment.

The Grace of God upon the church has indeed multiplied in many ways leading to church growth. God has continued touching the hearts of men in the church through the senior pastor who has labored faithfully in teaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ. The leadership of the senior pastor has been recognized by the national leadership of the church and he was therefore ordained as a Reverend and later a Bishop. All these responsibilities are a testament to the centrality of the church in the affairs of not only its catchment area but also in the national agenda of the church.

Vision

A church committed to preaching the gospel of Jesus Christ in order to draw men to the house of God while transforming them into a royal priesthood abounding in all blessings.

Mission

To teach the word of God to all men so that they may attain the knowledge of truth and live by it.

Location

The church is located in Kenya, Rift Valley, Bomet county, Kaplong trading centre along Kaplong-Bomet highway. The area is barely cool and wet with most residents practicing livestock and crop farming as their main economic activities. A few people rely on small business as their source of livelihood. The church is also surrounded by learning institutions such as Kaplong Boys’ secondary school, Kaplong Girls’ National school among others. In the neighborhood also is St. Clares Kaplong Hospital. This kind of environment leads to a potential catchment area for church membership.
